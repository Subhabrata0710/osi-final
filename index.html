Join us as we explore the frontiers of bone-implant integration, advanced prosthetic solutions, and the latest surgical techniques.

Distinguished Speakers

Keynote Speaker

Dr Frank Schwarz

Prof. Dr Frank Schwarz... Prof. Dr. Frank Schwarz is one of the world’s leading authorities on peri-implantitis management and regenerative implant therapies. As Chairman of the Department of Oral Surgery and Implantology at Goethe University Frankfurt, Germany, he has pioneered evidence-based surgical approaches—including reconstructive techniques, implantoplasty, and advanced bone regeneration—to save implants affected by infection and bone loss. A key figure in global guidelines (EAO, Osteology Foundation), his lectures deliver practical, proven strategies to improve long-term implant success and handle complex complications. Attend his keynote to master the latest in peri-implant health and turn challenging cases into predictable outcomes.

Keynote Speaker

Dr. Yoshihito Naito

Dr. Yoshihito Naito... Dr. Yoshihito Naito, DDS, PhD, is a leading expert in short implants, esthetic implant rehabilitation, and biomaterial-optimized osseointegration. Affiliated with Tokushima University, Japan, he has published extensively on the clinical success, radiographic stability, and patient satisfaction of short implants in challenging atrophic ridges—offering reliable alternatives to complex grafting procedures. His evidence-based insights help clinicians achieve excellent functional and esthetic results with minimal invasiveness. Don’t miss his keynote to discover practical considerations for short implants and advanced strategies that simplify treatment while maximizing predictability and patient acceptance.
